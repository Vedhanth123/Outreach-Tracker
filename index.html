How this works
For every contact, the system tracks 5 touchpoints: connection request → accept → Day-1 message → Day-4 bump → Day-8 email → Day-14 close. Items show here when they're due today or overdue. Mark them done in the Contacts table or open a contact for full timeline control.
60-Day Outreach Roadmap
// 8 WEEKS · 200 CONTACTS · 1-2 OFFERS
Week 1 · Day 1-7
Build the List
Identify 50 target people. 5 personalized connection requests/day starting Day 3. Use LinkedIn searches from the playbook.
Week 2 · Day 8-14
Engage & Send
10 requests/day. Engage (like/comment) on target posts before DMing. Day-1 follow-up messages on accepted connections.
Week 3 · Day 15-21
Bumps & Email
Day-4 bump messages. Find emails (Hunter.io). Re-engage Deloitte and warm leads. Apply via job boards in parallel.
Week 4 · Day 22-28
Email Touch + Close
Day-8 cold emails. Day-14 "soft close" messages. First interview prep cycle. Audit what's converting; double down.
Week 5 · Day 29-35
Interview Mode
Active interview pipeline. New outreach drops to 5/day to manage cognitive load. Refine resumes by interviewer feedback.
Week 6 · Day 36-42
Iterate
Re-engage cold leads from Week 1-2. Apply to roles posted since last review. Bug bounty submissions to add portfolio weight.
Week 7 · Day 43-49
Closing Conversations
Final-round interviews. Negotiation prep. Keep pipeline warm — don't pause outreach until you've signed.
Week 8 · Day 50-60
Land
Decision time. If no offer yet: re-engage Day-60 cold leads with new context (new cert, project, role change). System restart.
